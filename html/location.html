Location



  • We are located 10 minutes off Route 6, Exit 7 in West Yarmouth
  • The house sits less than a half mile to Colonial Acres Beach at the end of the street - only a 5 minute walk!
  • Half a mile to Route 28, convenient to everything
  • This quiet neighborhood is great for an evening stroll or an early morning jog
  • Quick drive to public beaches with amenities, Seagull Beach, Seaview Beach, Kalmus Beach Park


DIRECTIONS

  • Take Route 6 to exit 7
  • Left off exit onto Willow Street
  • Straight through first light past Higgens Crowell Road
  • Take left onto Camp Street (just before the gas station)
  • Follow a couple miles to Route 28
  • Right onto Route 28
  • Immediate left onto Wendward Way (across from Golden Boy Seafood)
  • We are just a half mile down on the left
