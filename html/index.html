We are located...

...A little ways in from Route 28...

...A little ways in from Colonial Acres Beach on Lewis Bay.


The moment you pull into the driveway of A Little Ways Inn you are officially on vacation. In fact, this will become your home away from home for the duration of your stay.

Enjoy all the comforts and amenities you left behind, without the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Inside are three bedrooms to sleep six, fully-equipped kitchen and updated bath. Family room includes comfy seating, TV and DVD player.

The house sits on an open half-acre lot with fully fenced in back yard. All family members, including kids and dogs will love the open space to run and play safely.
